\abstract{
	Academic writing does not have to be drab, and neither does the academic writing template.
	The template should elevate the writing, not diminish it, but in academia, form normally follows function.
	The { \upshape Elegant } template is my attempt to rectify that shortcoming.
	I designed it as a no-frills but elegant template, built on the standard article template.
	At its core, three principles: simplicity, readability and aesthetic.
	May this template elevate your writing.
}
